History of The Argo Ship

Argo was constructed by the shipwright Argus, and its crew were especialy protected by the goddess Hera...
According to a variety of sources of the legend, Argo was said to have been planned or constructed with the help of Athena.
According to certain sources, Argo was the first ship to sail the seas.
